The drylab, by Turing Biotech

Biology needs to, and is becoming, more and more quantitative. This is because intuitive, qualitative reasoning is not enough to make sense of complex biological systems. Mathematical and computational models are more and more needed to enable impactful biological research: the data alone is too rich, complex and noisy.

However, today, it requires scientists a lot of skills, expertise and knowledge to define a meaningful mathematical model of a biological system, to write code to simulate it, and to analyse its results. And still, even then it takes an important amount of time and coding (and debugging) to turn a model drawn on paper to actual working code.

At Turing Biotech, we really want to change this. The drylab is our first contribution towards this goal. Try it, and tell us what you think !
